About us

We are a team of 5 aspirational students looking to break the boundaries and limitations of attending concerts.
We call our project « Transcend » as we are meaning to break through the physical boundaries in attending events physically, which may be saving venue space, transportation costs and above all … MONEY.
How many concerts have you dreamed of going to but were never able to attend because it was too far or too expensive ? We’re here to remedy this problem through our groundbreaking Virtual Reality technology.
